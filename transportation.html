Our Service

Safe, Reliable Transportation

Our fleet serves all neighborhoods around the school. Every bus is equipped with the latest safety features and driven by trained professionals.

GPS Tracking

Every bus is tracked live so parents always know where their child is at any time.

Safety Cameras

Security cameras installed inside and outside every bus for full protection.

Seat Belts

All seats are fitted with seat belts. Student safety is our number one priority.

Certified Drivers

All drivers undergo background checks, medical tests, and regular safety training.

Regular Maintenance

Buses are serviced weekly to ensure they are always roadworthy and reliable.

Parent Alerts

Parents receive SMS notifications when the bus departs and arrives at school.
