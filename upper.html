About Us

About Upper Primary

At Lead International School (LIS), the Upper Primary level marks the stage where learners develop stronger reasoning skills, discipline, independence, and confidence in expressing their ideas.

They are guided by trained teachers who combine modern teaching methods with real-life learning experiences to prepare every child for the next level.

Our Upper Primary learners are encouraged to ask questions, think critically, work in teams, and take ownership of their own learning journey.
