About Us

Our Little World

🏫 Crèche · Nursery · KG

Our Preschool has two main departments managed by their respective heads — the Crèche & Nursery and Kindergarten.

Caregivers give children free play time while closely monitoring their development. Every child's needs are catered for — changing time, potty training, meals, nap time and more.

Children learn how to socialize, make friends, and share. Independence, self-control, and cooperation are nurtured daily. Each child learns at their own pace.

As early as 6:00 a.m., our caregivers are in to welcome the children. After-school care is also available. Our staff are experienced, skilled, and treat every child as their own. 💛
