About Us

Our Lower Primary

Primary 1 – 3

At Lead International School (LIS), our Lower Primary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, creativity, and social development.

We provide a safe, loving, and engaging learning environment where children explore, imagine, and grow confidently every single day.

Our experienced teachers use interactive and child-centered methods to ensure that every learner — regardless of pace — is supported and celebrated.
