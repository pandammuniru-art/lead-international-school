About Our Lower Primary

At Lead International School (LIS), our Lower Primary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, creativity, and social development. We provide a safe, loving, and engaging learning environment where children explore, imagine, and grow confidently.

What We Do

Classroom Learning

Interactive lessons that help young learners understand reading, writing, and numbers in a fun way.

Creative Activities

Art, drawing, singing, and storytelling to help children express themselves creatively.

Group Learning

Teamwork and collaboration through group projects and playful activities.

Outdoor Learning

Outdoor playtime, exploration, and sports to build confidence and physical development.
