About Upper Primary

At Lead International School (LIS), the Upper Primary level marks the stage where learners develop stronger reasoning skills, discipline, independence, and confidence in expressing their ideas. They are guided by trained teachers who combine modern teaching methods with real‑life learning experiences.

Programs & Activities

Core Academic Subjects

Maths, English, Science, ICT, Social Studies, French — taught with advanced problem‑solving, discussions, and projects.

Research & ICT Skills

Learners engage in typing, online research, presentations, coding basics, and digital creativity.

Talent Development

Clubs such as STEM, Debate, Creative Writing, Arts & Crafts, Music and Leadership programs.

Sports & Fitness

Football, athletics, indoor games, endurance training and teamwork exercises to build healthy confidence.

Upper Primary Curriculum Overview

  • English Language: Grammar, comprehension, writing & public speaking.
  • Mathematics: Problem-solving, geometry, algebra foundations.
  • Science: Experiments, observation skills, analysis.
  • ICT: Computer basics, typing, coding introduction.
  • Social Studies: Culture, governance, environment.
  • French: Basic communication and vocabulary building.
