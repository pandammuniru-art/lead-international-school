About Our Programs

What We Offer

At Lead International School, we offer a diverse range of programs designed to cater to the holistic development of our students. Our curriculum is structured to provide a balance between academic excellence and extracurricular activities.

Early Childhood Education

Pre-K to Kindergarten

Nurturing young minds through play-based learning, creativity, and social development in a safe and loving environment.

Primary Education

Grades 1 – 6

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts for every primary school learner.

Secondary Education

Grades 7 – 12 (JHS)

Rigorous academic preparation including BECE coaching, leadership training, and career-driven skill development.

STEM Programs

Science · Technology · Engineering · Maths

Hands-on experiments, robotics, coding basics, and problem-solving sessions that spark innovation in every learner.

Arts & Humanities

Creative & Cultural Studies

Drama, music, visual arts, history, and literature — helping students find and express their unique voice and talent.

Sports & Physical Education

All Levels

Football, athletics, indoor games, and fitness training to build healthy bodies, discipline, and teamwork skills.

Extracurricular Clubs

After School Activities

Debate club, chess, creative writing, STEM club, music band, and more — something exciting for every student.

Leadership & Community Service

Character Development

Student council, mentorship programs, community outreach, and public speaking to develop responsible future leaders.

Cadet & More

Discipline · Respect · Order

Cadet training, discipline programs, and many more special activities designed to build character and responsibility.

Designed for Every Learner

Each program is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and social responsibility among our students — preparing them for success in all their future endeavors.
