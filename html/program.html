Our school Programs

At Lead International School, we offer a diverse range of programs designed to cater to the holistic development of our students. Our curriculum is structured to provide a balance between academic excellence and extracurricular activities.

  • Early Childhood Education (Pre-K to Kindergarten)
  • Primary Education (Grades 1-6)
  • Secondary Education (Grades 7-12)
  • STEM Programs (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)
  • Arts and Humanities
  • Sports and Physical Education
  • Extracurricular Clubs and Activities
  • Leadership and Community Service Programs
  • Cadet and many more...

Each program is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and social responsibility among our students, preparing them for success in their future endeavors.

About Us

We are dedicated to empowering students through education, innovation, and opportunities. Our goal is to make a lasting impact in every community we reach.
