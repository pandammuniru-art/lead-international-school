About LIS JHS

The Junior High School Department at Lead International School focuses on preparing students for the BECE and future academic challenges. At this stage, learners are trained to think critically, solve problems independently, communicate confidently, and build strong moral discipline.

Academic Programs

BECE Preparation

Intensive subject revision, mock exams, study groups, and exam coaching for excellence.

Advanced ICT & Research

Typing, coding basics, research tools, presentations, and digital literacy improvement.

STEM & Critical Thinking

Science experiments, robotics basics, mathematics drills, and logical reasoning development.

Leadership & Personal Growth

Debate club, student council, mentorship programs, public speaking and responsibility training.
