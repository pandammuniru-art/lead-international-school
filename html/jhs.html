The Junior High School Department at Lead International School focuses on preparing students for the BECE and future academic challenges.

At this stage, learners are trained to think critically, solve problems independently, communicate confidently, and build strong moral discipline.

Our JHS graduates consistently excel in national exams and go on to attend some of Ghana's finest secondary schools.
