Who We Are

Our Administration

Lead International School (LIS) is a co-educational, English-medium institution located in Pokuase, Accra. With over 20 years of providing holistic education, we have grown into a thriving community of over 2,500 learners, 150 dedicated teachers, and 100 non-teaching staff.

We provide a happy, safe, and secure environment where every child is encouraged to actively participate in their own learning journey. Our approach ensures that each learner develops at their own pace while being challenged to reach their fullest potential.

Our Vision

Where We're Going

Our vision is to be a preferred educational institution recognized for excellence in teaching and learning. We aspire to nurture creative, innovative, and critical thinkers who can actualize their aspirations and contribute positively to Ghana and the world.

  • Promote equal opportunity and social justice
  • Encourage lifelong access to education
  • Support economic growth and community prosperity
  • Create a secure, God-centered learning environment
Our Journey

How We Started

2002

The Beginning

Lead International School opened its doors with a bold vision — to provide world-class education right here in Pokuase, Accra.

2005

Growing Community

Student enrollment grew rapidly as LIS became known for its quality teaching, safe environment, and excellent results.

2012

Expansion

New classrooms, a science lab, and sports facilities were added to serve our growing community of learners.

Today

2500+ Strong

With over 2500 students and 150 teachers, LIS continues to lead and inspire the next generation of Ghanaian leaders.
